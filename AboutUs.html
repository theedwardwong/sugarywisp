About Us

Monin is a syrup company based in Bourges, France; which is selling syrups, liqueurs, and fruit purees.

Also, it is the leading supplier of flavoring ingredients to produce specialized beverages and gourmet culinary solutions.

The production units have located in three continents, over hundred employees working, and more than one hundred and forty-five which the product is available to sell.

Mainly, it is sold for commercial use such as bars, hotels, restaurants, cafes, luxury places and others.

Historically, it was family-owned and have been running for over a hundred years; It has three generations coming from the same family.

The founder of its company was Georges Monin; whom founded this company but the first syrup wasn’t made until 1920s.

Later in 1944, Georges Monin was passed away then his son, Paul Monin, took over the entire company several months later. He left his own wine production to pursuing and commercializing on syrups, building a network with traders in France entirely.

In 1996, Oliver Monin, Paul Monin’s son, instituted a manufacturing unit in Clearwater, Florida.

In 2018, he also expanding to the industrial productions in China, Malaysia, and America. Currently, Monin companies has their locations in North America, Australia, Asia, and France.
