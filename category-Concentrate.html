Quality Syrup with natural ingredient

Monin’s uses multiple recipe for its syrup to ensure its quality and fine taste preserve.

Habanero Lime

Monin’s Habanero Lime has a heat from the habanero but acidity from the lime. It is used in beers, mojitos, margaritas, bloody marys, and more to give a zing. Through the nose is fresh lime while the attack once drunk is citrusy with sweetness and the aftertaste is spicy.

RM50.00

Lemon Rantcho

Monin’s Lemon Rantcho is used for many things such as classic daiquiri, fruity drinks for a punch, and is also used for culinary. It is sugar free without its pulp and is 50% juice made from Sicily lemons. Through the nose is a balance smell of fresh lemon and lime while the attack once drunk is acidic from the freshly squeezed lime juice and the aftertaste is refreshing even though.

RM50.00

Lime Juice Cardial

Monin’s Lime Juice Cordial is used in Margaritas, Maï-Taïs and Gin & Tonics and is a mix of lime juice, lemon juice, and sugar which is 50% juice. Since it is a cordial, it has a low sugar content. Through the nose is a smell of fresh lemon juice while the attack once drunk is a balance of lemon and lime juice and the aftertaste is a balanced acidity and sweetness.

RM50.00

Lime Rantcho

Monin’s Lime Rantcho is used in Gin & Tonics, Margaritas, Caïpirinhas, and Mojitos for a zing of lime to taste. Through the nose is a smell of fresh lime while the attack once drunk is fresh lime juice with high acidity and the aftertaste sour.

RM55.00

Sweet & Sour

Monin’s Sweet & Sour mix is used in typical bars for cocktails, margarita, daiquiri, or Long Island iced tea. It is made from pure sugar, Sicilian lemons, & dashes of lime juice. Through the nose is a smell of lemon zest while the attack once drunk is pure fresh lemon juice from Sicily and the aftertaste is lemon astringent.

RM55.00
